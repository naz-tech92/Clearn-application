Networking

Design and maintain computer networks

1 Skill Overview

What is Networking?

Computer networking involves designing, implementing, and maintaining networks that connect computers and devices. It encompasses hardware, software, protocols, and security measures to ensure reliable communication and data transfer between systems.

Problems It Solves

Enables communication between devices, ensures secure data transmission, provides internet connectivity, supports cloud services, enables remote work capabilities, and maintains network performance and reliability.

Industries Where It's Used

  • Banking & Finance
  • Healthcare
  • Manufacturing
  • Government
  • Education
  • Telecommunications
