Medical Laboratory Science

What is Medical Laboratory Science?

Medical laboratory science involves analyzing bodily fluids, tissues, and other substances to detect diseases, monitor treatment effectiveness, and provide critical diagnostic information. Laboratory scientists use sophisticated equipment and techniques to perform tests that help physicians diagnose and treat patients.

Problems It Solves

Enables accurate disease diagnosis

Monitors treatment effectiveness

Detects infections and abnormalities

Supports blood transfusion safety

Industries Where It's Used

Hospitals Diagnostic Laboratories Clinics Blood Banks Research Institutions Government Health Agencies
