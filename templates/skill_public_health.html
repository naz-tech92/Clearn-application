What is Public Health?

Public health focuses on protecting and improving the health of communities through education, policy development, research, and disease prevention. It addresses population-level health issues and works to create environments that support healthy living.

Problems It Solves

Prevents disease outbreaks, promotes healthy lifestyles, addresses health disparities, develops public health policies, manages community health programs, and responds to health emergencies and pandemics.
