Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 27, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how CLearn collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you visit and use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal information

  • Name and email address submitted during account creation or contact requests.
  • Account credentials (stored securely as hashed values).
  • Profile preferences you choose to save.

Usage information

  • Device/browser details, IP address, and pages visited.
  • Session activity and interaction data used for analytics and platform improvements.

3. How We Use Information

  • Deliver and maintain platform features.
  • Personalize content and improve performance.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Detect abuse, enforce policies, and improve security.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for session management, preference storage, analytics, and reliability. You can control cookies in browser settings, but some features may not work correctly if disabled.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers that support hosting, analytics, authentication, and security operations, under contractual safeguards.

6. Data Security

We apply technical and organizational safeguards such as encrypted transport, secure configurations, and access controls. No online service can be guaranteed as completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, security, and legitimate business purposes.
